Research and Education Institute
--------------------------------

MapMentors is a research and education institute based in Nepal, dedicated to empowering students, researchers, and professionals by providing access to spatial technologies to solve real-world problems. Our mission is to guide individuals in leveraging geospatial data, mapping tools, and advanced technology to tackle challenges in various fields, including environmental conservation, urban planning, disaster management, agriculture, and more. Whether you are a student exploring spatial technology for the first time, a researcher working on a specific project, or a professional looking to apply geospatial solutions to your work, MapMentors connects you with experts and mentors to help you develop the skills and knowledge necessary to drive impactful change. Our Mission MapMentors aims to foster a community where individuals can access quality mentorship, resources, and training in spatial technology. We focus on supporting students and researchers in Nepal and beyond, guiding them on how to use tools such as Geographic Information Systems (GIS), remote sensing, spatial analysis, and mapping to solve complex problems and contribute to the development of their communities.
